Future of Fundamentals

We have found a novel way for value investment. We all love to trade in stock, bond or option of a publicly listed company. But our ride on the road to successful investments is not safe if we don't know the inner truths of how a company may perform in the future given a number of circumstances, internal or external to the company.

This is what this site is good at doing. We have built an excellent prediction algorithm. This algorithm has the ability to rummage through innumerable macro-economics and micro-economics data, the company and its competitors fundamentals and many more, then build a complex graph and come to a conclusion on how a company may end up in a future quarter, quarters or years.
